I have always been quite the afficiando of a multitude of delicious and delectible beers from around the world. I love beer and every variety that it comes in, even the cheap stuff! All I ask is that you help share my affinity for what Benjamin Franklin described as "proof that God loves us and wants us to be happy!". Why, I love beer so much that I am willing to learn how to brew and even brew you a beer! The purpose of this bitstarter is to help fund my pursuits to create the best beer, or your favorite beer, depending on how much you donate (wink wink).

I am going to be accepting payments in the form of bitcoins.

Pledging:

  • n00b donation: Pledge $10 equivalent in bitcoins or more:
      I will buy one six pack of your choice (even if it costs me more than the 10 dollars pledged). You will get a picture of me enjoying your beer of choice
  • Fledgling donation: Pledge $75 equivalent in bitcoins or more
      I will purchase one case of your beer of choice (cost permitting) and you will receive a Mr. Beer Premium Gold Edition Beer Kit as well as the n00b donation package.
  • Maester donation: Pledge $300 equivalent in bitcoins or more
      You will receive one case of your homebrewed delight (after the month or so long process and pending shipment to your state) as well as the n00b and fledgling packages).
